Programmed Adventure

Welcome to Programmed Adventure, a collaborative Choose Your Own Adventure story forum!

Available story titles are listed to the left. Each story will offer you choices on how you wish to proceed. There is a catch, however. You may reach a branch in the story that does not provide you with options. If you do, please tell us what you would do! Your suggestion may be added to the story. Stories are updated regularly and every option was suggested by readers like you. The story cannot continue without you.

Follow us on twitter @ChooseYrProgram to find out when we update a story!

If you would like to be creditted as one of our contributers in the case that we choose your suggestion, just include the way you would like to be creditted (name, twitter handle, tumblr url, email). We will not share the email you use to submit without your permission.

If you're interested in the code that went into creating this website, feel free to check it out on GitHub. All of this information is also available there in a README.txt file.

This website was created as a project for How to do Things With Literature at the University of Pittsburgh. Creater Katrina Dagenais can be found at katrinadagenais.github.io

Email us at programmedadventure@gmail.com

Every choice is thanks to our contributers!
